Good quality medical records are an essential component of safe and effective healthcare


    Here are five reasons why every person should monitor their personal health record:
    1. Save money.
    2. Ensure accuracy.
    3. Have more control.
    4. Provide information as needed.
    5. Stay organized.
Having your own medical history at your disposal puts you, the patient, back in a position of power to be responsible and informed of your own health status. When all is said and done, you’re the person with the most at stake.
